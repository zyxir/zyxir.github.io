How to say 0

  • zero

    Used to read the number by itself, in reading decimals, percentages, and phone numbers, and in some fixed expressions.

  • o (the letter name)

    Used to read years, addresses, times and temperatures.

  • nil

    Used to report sports scores.

  • nought

    Not used in USA.
